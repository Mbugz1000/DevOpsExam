Perfect for lunch

Our group of six ate lunch on Wednesday, ordering a variety of menu items. The bacon avo mayo sandwich on ciabatta bread was delicious, but the table favorite was the chicken, feta, and coriander crepe! The food was all beautifully presented and reasonably priced. The art gallery is full of exquisite pieces, and the grounds are perfectly kept. The biggest plus perhaps is the location: centrally located to both the elephant orphanage and the giraffe centre.
